Hi! I'm Alex, a junior studying Digital Media Design at the University of Pennsylvania's School of Engineering. In this major, I focus largely on computer science, and have additional electives for fine arts classes like computer modeling.

Outside of academics, I spend most of my time at The Daily Pennsylvanian, where I am the Digital Director. At the DP I oversee digital strategy and creative direction, and I manage seven digital departments, including web development, video, and design. I also work as a web developer at the DP, for which I work on project pages and full-scale apps. On the DP's previous editorial board, I was a design editor. I work regularly as a designer (in the past, graphic design, sound, and projections) on the productions of Penn's various theater companies. I am also a member of Penn's chapter of oSTEM.
