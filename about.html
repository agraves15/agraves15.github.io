Hi! I'm Alex, a sophomore studying Digital Media Design at the University of Pennsylvania's School of Engineering. In this major, I study both computer science and fine arts, with a focus ultimately in computer graphics.

Outside of academics, I spend most of my time at The Daily Pennsylvanian, where I am the Digital Director. At the DP I oversee digital strategy and creative direction, and I manage seven digital departments, including web development, analytics, and design. I work regularly as a designer (in the past, both sound and projections) on the productions of Penn's various theater companies. I am also a member of Penn's chapter of oSTEM.
