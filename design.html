From January to December 2016, I served as one of two design editors at The Daily Pennsylvanian. During that time I supervised the daily print layout, as well as led the art direction of longer-term projects.

Aside from my work at the DP, I love graphic design, and always take opportunities to create, from projections for theater productions to posters for clubs.
